MISSION

Welcome! This is a page where kids can come to explore and learn more about the importance of right nutrition and maintianing a healthy lifestyle. We believe that all kids deserve the chance for a happy and healthy education. The foundation for obtaining this education stems from proper nutrition and exercise. People must be healthy in order to have the energy to learn. Our goal is to educate kids with an interactive game about the importance of getting the right nutrition and maintaining a healthy lifestyle so that they can get the chance to learn. Stay educated. Be healthy.
